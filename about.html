OUR BACKGROUND

Chilambo General Trade Company Limited [CGTCL] is a Tanzanian private business entity chiefly dealing with e-waste management activities comprising of collection, transportation and storage of electrical and electronic waste [Ewaste].

Chilambo General Trade Company Limited formerly traded under the business name: Chilambo General Trade Company founded in 1990.

In 2012, the company decided to diversify its core business activities and formally started engaging in electrical and electronic waste management [e-waste].

CGTCL Pillars
Mission

To reach as many potential e-waste generators as possible countrywide, who are currently users of ICT assets, and link them with our e-waste management services to ensure joint responsibility of protecting our natural environment from e-waste pollution.

Vision

To be the lead e-waste management company in Tanzania with its firm commitment in ensuring that e-waste generation in the country is properly and safely managed to safeguard the environment and nature well being.
